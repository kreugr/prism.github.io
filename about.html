We Are Prism.

Throughout the past five years, Prism has developed and established its online media property, Prism.com, as the most reputable, visited and recognized publication in the world of Electronic Dance Music. With over 8 million monthly visitors, Prism.com has continuously sought to deliver the most up to date, accurate and poignant news related to the EDM scene. Based out of D3, Dubai with an ever-growing staff of journalists, photographers, and videographers from around the country Prism.com has been capable of creating and maintaining unparalleled coverage of this ever-growing industry.
