Tommorowland 2020.

Tomorrowland is a Belgian electronic dance music festival held in Boom, Belgium. Tomorrowland was first held in 2005 and has since become one of the world's largest and most notable music festivals. It now stretches over 2 weekends and usually sells out in minutes. This years Tomorrowland chose "The reflection of love" as its theme, which was already secretly revealed in the 2019 mainstage.

  • Friday, July 17, 2020
  • 1:00Pm - 2:30AM
  • 1600 Arena, Boom, Belgium

Electric Forest.

Electric Forest is a 4-day, one-weekend, multi-genre event with a focus on electronic and jam band genres, held in Rothbury, Michigan, at the Double JJ Resort. Electric Forest, which debuted in 2011, is co-produced by Madison House Presents and Insomniac Events. Lineup for this years edition of Electric Forest includes To p artists of the EDM industry like Major Lazer, Flume, Petit Biscuit, Bassnectar, YBN Coradae etc.

  • THursday, June 25, 2020
  • 2:00PM - 1:30AM
  • Double JJ Resort, Rothbury, MI

Lollapalooza.

Lollapalooza is an annual four-day music festival based in Chicago, Illinois at Grant Park. Performances include but are not limited to alternative rock, heavy metal, hip hop, and electronic music. Lollapalooza has also provided a platform for non-profit and political groups and various visual artists. The four-day music festival in Grant Park hosts an estimated 400,000 people each year and sells out annually.

  • Thursday, July 30, 2020
  • 12:00PM - 12:00AM
  • Grant Park, Chicago, IL

Amsterdam Music Festival.

The Amsterdam Music Festival (AMF) is an electronic dance music (EDM) event that takes place during the Amsterdam Dance Event mid-October in Amsterdam, Netherlands.
